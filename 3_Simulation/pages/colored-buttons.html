üé® Colored Button Organization

Easy Design 20 min

Use color-coding to organize Stream Deck buttons by function, priority, or workflow category. Colors provide instant visual recognition, reducing cognitive load and improving speed during operations.

  • Software: Stream Deck software with button customization
  • Design Tool: Optional - Photoshop, Figma, or Canva for custom icons
  • Color Palette: Defined color scheme for your workflow
  • Time: 15-30 minutes for initial setup
  • Knowledge: Basic color theory helpful but not required

üìù Implementation Steps

1 Define Your Color System

Choose 4-6 colors that will represent different categories or priority levels in your workflow.

2 Categorize Your Buttons

List all buttons and assign each to a color category based on function or priority.

3 Apply Colors Consistently

In Stream Deck software:

  • Select button ‚Üí Style ‚Üí Background Color
  • Or use custom icons with colored backgrounds
  • Maintain consistency across all profiles

4 Add Visual Contrast

Ensure text/icons have good contrast against background colors for readability.

5 Test and Refine

Use your setup for a few days and adjust colors based on what works best for your workflow.

üõ†Ô∏è Steps to Build

Phase 1: Design or Download

  1. 1
    Measure Your Stream Deck XL
    Use calipers to measure the exact dimensions including the rubber feet and USB-C port location. The XL is approximately 182 √ó 112 √ó 21 mm.
  2. 2
    Choose a Design Approach
    Option A: Download a pre-made STL from Thingiverse or Printables ‚Äî search for "Stream Deck XL mount".
    Option B: Design your own in Fusion 360 or TinkerCAD for a fully custom fit.
  3. 3
    Add Cable Channel
    Ensure your design includes a 12 mm wide channel at the rear for the USB-C cable to exit cleanly.

Phase 2: Print

  1. 4
    Slice the Model
    Import the STL into your slicer (Cura, PrusaSlicer, Bambu Studio). Recommended settings: 0.2 mm layer height, 20% infill, 3 perimeters.
  2. 5
    Print the Mount
    Print with the flat base down. Use supports if tilt angle exceeds 45¬∞. Typical print time: 4‚Äì8 hours depending on size and infill.
  3. 6
    Post-Process
    Remove supports, sand rough edges with 220-grit sandpaper, then smooth with 400-grit. Optionally, apply a coat of spray paint or clear coat.

Phase 3: Install

  1. 7
    Test Fit
    Place the Stream Deck XL into the mount. It should sit snugly without wobble. Sand tight spots if needed.
  2. 8
    Route the Cable
    Connect the USB-C cable and route it through the cable channel. Secure with adhesive cable clips if needed.
  3. 9
    Position on Desk
    Place the mounted Stream Deck at a comfortable reach distance. Add anti-slip pads to the bottom of the mount to prevent sliding.

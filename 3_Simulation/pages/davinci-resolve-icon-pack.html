‚öôÔ∏è How to Install an Icon Pack

Step 1 ‚Äì Download or create your icon pack. Icons should be 72√ó72 or 144√ó144 pixel PNG files with a transparent or dark background.
Step 2 ‚Äì Open the Elgato Stream Deck application.
Step 3 ‚Äì Select the button you want to customise. In the button settings panel, click on the icon/image area.
Step 4 ‚Äì Choose "Set from File‚Ä¶" and navigate to the downloaded icon PNG.
Step 5 ‚Äì Repeat for each button. Organise icons into folders by category (Edit, Playback, Colour, etc.) for faster setup.

üí° Pro Tip ‚Äì Icon Size

Stream Deck buttons render at 72√ó72 pixels (standard) or 144√ó144 (XL/retina). Design at 144√ó144 for the sharpest result ‚Äî the software will downscale automatically for smaller devices.

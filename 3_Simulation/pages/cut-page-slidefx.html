ü§ñ SlideFX Extension ‚Äî What It Does

The SlideFX plugin (shown as purple robot icons on all 4 knobs) extends DaVinci Resolve editing with AI-assisted slide transitions:

  • Rotate any knob to scrub through transition styles in real time
  • Click a knob to apply the selected transition to the clip under the playhead
  • Each knob can be configured for a different transition category (dissolve, wipe, push, slide)
  • The LCD touch strip updates to show the active transition name and duration
  • Works with both the Cut page and Edit page in DaVinci Resolve
üí° Tip: Map each knob to a different transition family ‚Äî Knob 1: Dissolves, Knob 2: Wipes, Knob 3: Pushes, Knob 4: Custom slides. Rotate to browse, click to apply.

Phase 1: Create the Profile

  1. 3

    Drag DaVinci Resolve plugin actions for Insert, Overwrite, and Fit to Fill to buttons 2‚Äì4 in the top row.

  1. 5

    Click each of the 4 touch strip areas. Assign: JKL shuttle, Select Clip, Go to Marker, and Timeline View respectively.
  2. 6

    Go to the Elgato Marketplace in Stream Deck ‚Üí search SlideFX ‚Üí Install. Restart Stream Deck software.
  3. 7
    Assign SlideFX to All 4 Knobs
    Drag the SlideFX action to each of the 4 dial slots. Configure each knob's category (dissolve, wipe, push, slide).

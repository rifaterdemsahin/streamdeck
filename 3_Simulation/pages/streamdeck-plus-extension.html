üõ†Ô∏è Setup Steps

  1. 1
    Unpack the Extension Stand
    Remove the extension stand from its packaging. It comes as a single piece ‚Äî no assembly needed.
  2. 2
    Disconnect the Stream Deck +
    Unplug the USB-C cable from the back of your Stream Deck + to make attachment easier.
  3. 3
    Attach the Stand
    Align the Stream Deck + with the extension stand's cradle and press down until it clicks/snaps into place. The fit should be snug with no wobble.
  4. 4
    Route the Cable
    Reconnect the USB-C cable and route it through the stand's built-in cable channel. This keeps the wire hidden behind the device.
  5. 5
    Position on Desk
    Place the assembled unit at a comfortable reach distance. The elevated angle should give you clear visibility of all LCD keys, dials, and the touch strip.
  6. 6
    Verify Stability
    Press buttons and turn dials firmly to ensure the stand holds steady. The rubber pads should prevent any sliding on the desk surface.
